Сlassic lemonade

"The good old classics"

a refreshing drink for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • water — 2.5 liters
  • soda — 1.5 liters
  • Lemon — 4 pcs.
  • mint (sprig) — 3 pieces
  • Sugar — 1.5 cups

Recipe instructions

  1. First you need to make syrup. Pour the sugar into a saucepan and add a glass of water. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved in water.
  2. Three lemons are washed, cut in half and squeeze the juice out of them.
  3. The resulting lemon juice is poured into a three-liter jar and sweet syrup.
  4. Fill with drinking water (almost to the neck of the jar).
  5. Add 10-15 leaves of washed mint.
  6. Wash and cut the last lemon into circles and send it to the jar.
  7. We put the lemonade in the refrigerator for one hour so that it is infused and cooled down.
  8. That's it, your traditional summer lemonade is ready! Pour it into glasses and enjoy it!
