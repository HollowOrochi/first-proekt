Pear lemonade

"I think it will be more adequate than that piemonade..."

a refreshing drink for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • water — 2.5 liters
  • soda — 1.5 liters
  • pear — 1 kg
  • lemon — 1 piece
  • mint (sprig) — 3 pieces
  • of honey — to taste

Recipe instructions

  1. Wash the pears, slice them, and remove the core. Put it in a saucepan, pour lemon juice, add mint, cover with water and put on medium heat. Bring to a boil and cook for 30 minutes until tender.
  2. Cool to room temperature, add honey to taste and pour in the mineral water.
  3. To serve, put ice, mint and lemonade in a glass.
